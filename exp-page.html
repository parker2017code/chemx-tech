Applications

Electrochemistry

Hydrogen Electrocatalysts

Optimize OER/HER active layers and support matrices. Screen Pt-Co-Ni, Ru-based, and high-entropy alloy composition spaces to accelerate PEM fuel cell development.

Semiconductors

BEOL Contact Resistance

Map contact resistance across barrier metal and adhesion promoter composition gradients. Reduce qualification cycles for back-end interconnect materials.

Coatings

Hard Nitride Coatings

Combinatorial HEA nitride screening. CrAlN, TiVAlN, and complex ternary systems. XRD phase mapping, nanoindentation hardness, adhesion across the full gradient.

Energy Storage

Solid-State Battery Interfaces

Composition screening for cathode/electrolyte and anode/electrolyte interfaces. Ionic conductivity and electrochemical stability mapping across ternary spaces.
