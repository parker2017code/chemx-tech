Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide a better user experience.

2. Our Cookie Usage

The xemx-materials.com website is designed to be a simple, fast-loading informational site. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or marketing cookies.

2.1 Essential Cookies

We may use minimal essential cookies only if technically necessary for:

  • Maintaining your session on the website
  • Remembering your navigation preferences (e.g., menu state)
  • Ensuring website security

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and do not require consent.

2.2 No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics services
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Heatmap or session recording tools

3. Browser Settings

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only certain types of cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Be notified when a cookie is being set

For instructions, visit your browser's help documentation:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

4. Privacy Considerations

Blocking cookies will not affect your ability to browse our website or submit contact inquiries. However, some features may not function optimally if essential cookies are disabled.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what cookies are being used and for what purpose
  • Control whether cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies at any time
  • Contact us with questions about our cookie practices
